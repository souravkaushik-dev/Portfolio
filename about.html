Hi again, I 'm Sourav Kaushik.

As a developer, I enjoy experimenting with animations, prototyping new ideas, and optimizing performance. I believe great software is dynamic—it should evolve and adapt, staying in tune with user needs. Whether integrating APIs or refining interactions, I focus on delivering polished and functional experiences.

Outside of coding, I’m always exploring new tech trends, fine-tuning my skills, and brainstorming my next project. I have a deep appreciation for clean UI, well-executed software architecture, and the potential of AI in development. When I’m not building apps, I’m probably learning something new or refining an existing project.
