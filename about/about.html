/ 02The Process
my approach to work

My approach to work starts with carefully thinking and designing the app on paper. I outline all its features in a notebook, ensuring every detail is considered before moving to development. I then use Android Studio to build the app and bring it to life. For a smooth, bug-free experience and to refine the UI, I rely on tools like ChatGPT and Gemini to enhance the overall quality and user experience.
